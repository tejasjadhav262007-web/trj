History

From our founding to a future-ready community

Established in 2006, Elite Academy began as a neighborhood school with a bold mission: to offer modern education grounded in values. Over two decades, our campus has expanded to include smart classrooms, dedicated labs, a resource-rich library, arts studios and sports fields. We continue evolving with the latest teaching practices and technology while preserving a strong community focus.
